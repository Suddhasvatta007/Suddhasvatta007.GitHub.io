Short Bio

I am a Computer Science Ph.D. scholar with a combined eight years of professional experience: four years as a software engineer in the banking and finance industry, followed by over four years of research, mentoring, and teaching in software engineering. My dissertation contributions are twofold: 1) mathematically defining regression testing in the context of agile environments and 2) developing business value-driven regression test selection algorithms for agile environments.

I developed an innovative, market-ready product: an intelligent genetic algorithm-based tool designed for business-value-driven regression test selection within agile CI/CD pipelines. This lightweight yet powerful solution intelligently selects requirements for regression testing aligned directly with business objectives, ensuring efficient use of resources and significantly enhancing delivery speed and reliability. This product-focused approach demonstrates my ability to identify real-world problems,conceptualize targeted solutions, and successfully deliver impactful software products.

My expertise also comprehensively covers Machine Learnning Operations (see paper below) and the entire software development life cycle—from design through deployment in highly regulated banking and finance environments built using mainframes. Thus, by leveraging my deep understanding of legacy financial systems coupled with my strong foundation of AWS cloud-native services, I have proposed architecture in my recent white papers (see below) to transform these mainframe systems seamlessly into secure, scalable AWS cloud architectures, thus affirming my position as a thought leader and expert solution architect.

Further distinguishing my profile are the leadership, mentoring, and management skills honed through my doctoral journey, including leading diverse research projects to successful outcomes. My proven ability to navigate complex technical environments, drive innovation, and effectively manage collaborative projects ensures my readiness to contribute strategically and technically to challenging roles at the intersection of software engineering, agile, and cloud solution architecture.
